# Quick Start

This guide explains the basic steps to get started with our service.

## Create Account

1. Visit the signup page
2. Enter your email address
3. Set your password

## Initial Setup

After creating your account, complete the following setup:

- Enter profile information
- Review notification settings
- Invite team members (optional)

## Next Steps

Once you've completed the basic setup, check out the [Feature Guide](../features/overview.md).
